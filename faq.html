    We believe that all foods can be both hearty and obtain a piquant taste,
    that can be achieved in a multitude of ways.

    We were heartbroken when our peers could not enjoy the same rich and
    delicious flavors as us, and thus wanted to achieve equality.


    As a company that believes in both health and happiness, we will allow you
    to virtually fill out a form where you can apply which allergies you have.

    If you do not own an electronic device, consult the waiter or waitress and
    they will take note of any allergies.

    We will provide suitable alternatives to address your needs.

    Currently, we do not have a rewards program that's available to the public,
    but we are working on one currently and it will be released very soon.


    You can consult either the menu or call the restaurant to get
    recommendations for foods without products you are allergic
    to.
