Farm-to-Table

We always source our ingredients directly from trusted local farms. To ensure

we can provide the highest quality food for our guests, we verify these farms

personally. All ingredients are grown without added GMOs and have minimal

added substances.


Ingredients are grown without environmental damage, such as chemical runoff,

nutrient depletion, and over-farming. It is a pleasure to support local,

environmentally friendly farms.


Preparation

Our ingredients are always fresh before cooking, and we handle them

with the utmost care to respect the efforts of the local farmers

who made it possible for us to serve them to you.


Any damaged or unsatisfactory ingredients are composted or donated to nearby

farms or gardening centers for use as fertilizer. We make sure not to

waste anything in our pursuit of providing the best possible service.


Sustainability

In order to make it more sustainable for the environment, the farms we source

our ingredients from don't engage in the use of pesticides. (This is

done to prevent runoff from harming other areas to preserve the integrity

of the soil)


Alongside this, we also use recyclable materials for kitchen equipment and to-go

boxes and cups, as well as in other places. Our aim is to be as environmentally

friendly as possible while still offering the best service there is. We hope

people who want to save the earth can enjoy our food for a long time to come.
